The history

For over 150 years, White Lotus has been more than a destination — it’s been a feeling. Hidden away from the noise of the world, this timeless retreat began as a simple coastal haven and blossomed into a place where stories unfold and memories linger. Artists found their muse here, lovers found their moment, and travelers found their peace. With every sunrise over the ocean and every candlelit evening, White Lotus continues to whisper the promise of escape, elegance, and belonging.

About Us

Founded in 1875, White Lotus began as a secluded coastal retreat for aristocrats seeking tranquility and natural beauty. Over the decades, it evolved into a symbol of refined luxury, blending classic charm with modern sophistication. From hosting royal banquets in the early 1900s to welcoming artists, dreamers, and travelers from around the world, White Lotus has stood as a sanctuary of elegance and serenity. Our legacy is built on unforgettable moments, exquisite hospitality, and the promise that every guest becomes part of our enduring story.
